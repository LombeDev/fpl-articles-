About the Platform

Kopala FPL is an independent dashboard designed to enhance the Fantasy Premier League experience with real-time data and scouting insights across the world's major leagues.

Our mission is to provide managers with a tactical command center, offering live scores, player statistics, and mini-league analysis in one seamless interface.

Disclaimer: This platform is not officially affiliated with the Premier League or Fantasy Premier League, This is an independent dashboard designed to enhance the Fantasy Premier League experience with real time data and scouting insights.
